Statistics

You can see the statistics of our evaluations up to this point, after running
our DNS X-Ray tool on your network. To perform the evaluation go to "X-Ray Your DNS"
tab. For explanation on the tests that will be performed see "Home"

This page presents results of DNS resolution platforms that used our tool thus far. The results are
collected since beginning 2017 and are stored in a database. Each time DNS X-ray is evaluated on
a new platform, the results are added to the database, and the graphs are updated to reflect the
data from the new evaluation.
